01 — The Service

What Sharp by Dylan provides

Sharp by Dylan provides hand sharpening of knives and scissors using Japanese whetstones. Results vary depending on the condition, steel quality, and original geometry of the blade.

Sharpening does not restore blades with structural damage — including broken tips, deep chips, or severe warping — to new condition, though improvement is typical in most cases.

02 — Pre-existing Damage

Condition at the time of collection

Blades are accepted on the basis that any pre-existing damage — including chips, cracks, bent tips, corrosion, or thinned spines — is noted at collection where visible.

Sharp by Dylan is not responsible for pre-existing damage. If you believe damage occurred during sharpening, contact me within 48 hours of return and I'll assess it fairly.

ⓘ

Tip: If your blade has known damage, mention it at booking or collection so it can be noted before work begins.

03 — Satisfaction Guarantee

What the guarantee actually means

If you're not satisfied with the result, I'll resharpen at no charge. This is a genuine practical commitment — not a guarantee of any specific outcome.

It does not cover dissatisfaction arising from the inherent limitations of a blade, such as low-quality steel, excessive wear, or expectations that exceed what the blade's geometry or condition allows.

04 — Turnaround

Timeframes and rush orders

Standard turnaround is 24 hours from collection. This is a target, not a guaranteed deadline — delays can occur, and you'll be notified promptly if they do.

Rush orders are completed on a best-effort basis and are subject to schedule availability at the time of booking.
