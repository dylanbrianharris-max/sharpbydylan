About

The craft behind the edge.

I'm Dylan — a Sydney-based knife sharpening specialist who's spent the last year obsessing over the art of whetstone sharpening. What started as a side interest became a genuine craft.

Anyone can do a patch-job — I reinvent your blade.

Using professional-grade Naniwa Japanese ceramic whetstones across multiple grits, every knife is finished on a leather strop loaded with chromium oxide for a mirror-polished, razor-sharp result. No electric grinders, no heat damage — just hand sharpening done right.

Sharp by Dylan operates across Northern Sydney, including the Lower North Shore, Upper North Shore, and surrounding suburbs.

My Sharpening Kit

The Naniwa stones and tools behind every edge — each grit serves a specific stage in the sharpening process.

  • Naniwa ceramic 400 grit — Primary Bevel Setting
  • Naniwa ceramic 1200 grit — Edge Refinement
  • Naniwa ceramic 3000 grit — Edge Perfection
  • Combination 120/240 stone — Repair and Reprofiling
  • Naniwa Nagura 600 — Stone Conditioning
  • Leather Strop + Chromium Oxide — Mirror Polishing

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about Sydney knife sharpening — process, pricing, and what to expect.

Why get my knives sharpened?
+

Why replace when your knives can be sharper than new for a quarter of the price?

Performance: a sharp knife makes cooking easy and enjoyable.

Safety: dull knives require more force, leading to more slips and accidents.

Why Sharp by Dylan?
+

Personal attention to detail: one person, one standard, every blade.

Whetstone only — no heat-generating grinders that damage steel.

Door-to-door — most Sydney sharpening services make you drop off and collect yourself.

Do you offer pickup and delivery across Sydney?
+
Yes — free pickup and delivery for orders of 5 or more knives across the Lower North Shore. For smaller orders or locations further out, a travel charge may apply — always confirmed before collection.
How long does knife sharpening take?
+
Most orders are returned within 24 hours. Same-day rush service is available for an additional $10 charge when scheduling permits.
What blades can you sharpen in Sydney?
+
Western and Japanese kitchen knives, chef's knives, cleavers, hunting knives, paring knives, chisels, and scissors. Serrated, circular, and power tool blades are not serviced. Not sure? Just ask.
How do I pay?
+
Payment via Eftpos (card tap), cash, bank transfer (BSB/Acc), or PayID on delivery. An invoice is sent when knives are returned.
Will sharpening damage my expensive knives?
+
No. Only water-saturated Japanese ceramic whetstones (Naniwa Abrasives) are used — no grinding wheels or belts that generate heat and damage steel. Safe for all knives including premium Japanese and German brands like Global, Wüsthof, and Shun.
