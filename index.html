About

The craft behind the edge.

I'm Dylan — a Sydney-based uni student who's spent the last year obsessing over the art of whetstone sharpening. What started as a side interest became a genuine craft.

Anyone can do a patch-job — I reinvent your blade.

Using professional-grade Japanese ceramic whetstones across multiple grits, every knife is finished on a leather strop loaded with chromium oxide for a mirror-polished, razor-sharp result.

Sharp by Dylan operates across the Sydney metro area. Pickup and delivery included for orders of 5+ knives.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about the process, pricing, and what to expect.

Why get my knives sharpened?
+

Why replace when your knives can be sharper than new for a quarter of the price?

Performance: a sharp knife makes cooking easy (and fun). Trust me.

Safety: dull knives require more force, leading to more slips, leading to less fingers.

Why Sharp by Dylan?
+

Personal attention to detail: one person, one standard, every blade.

Whetstone only — no heat-generating grinders that damage steel.

Door-to-door — most places make you drop off and collect yourself.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?
+
Yes — free pickup and delivery for orders of 3 or more knives across the Sydney metro area. For smaller orders, a collection charge may apply.
How long does sharpening take?
+
Most orders are returned within 24 hours. Same-day rush service is available for an additional charge when scheduling permits.
What blades can you sharpen?
+
Western and Japanese kitchen knives, chef's knives, cleavers, fillet knives, paring knives, and scissors. I don't sharpen serrated, circular or power tool blades.
How do I pay?
+
Payment via Eftpos (card tap), cash, bank transfer (BSB/Acc), or PayID on delivery. I'll send an invoice when I return your knives.
Will sharpening damage my expensive knives?
+
No. I only use water-saturated Japanese ceramic whetstones (Naniwa Abrasives) — no grinding wheels or belts that generate heat and damage steel. I've sharpened hundreds of knives of all descriptions, and not only do I avoid damage, I eliminate previous damage too.
